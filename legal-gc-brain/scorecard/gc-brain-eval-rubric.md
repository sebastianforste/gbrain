# GC Brain Evaluation Rubric

Use this rubric to evaluate whether a legal brain answer is suitable for senior legal review.

## Scoring

Score each answer from 0 to 5 on each dimension.

| Dimension | Question |
| --- | --- |
| Source grounding | Are key claims tied to source material? |
| Legal issue spotting | Does the answer identify the relevant legal and governance issues? |
| Business usefulness | Does the answer help a GC, product counsel or operator decide what to do next? |
| Gap analysis | Does the answer clearly state what is missing, stale or uncertain? |
| Escalation logic | Does the answer identify who must approve or review the issue? |
| Risk calibration | Is the risk level proportionate and commercially realistic? |
| Actionability | Are next steps concrete and owner-oriented? |
| Boundary discipline | Does the system avoid pretending to be the final legal decision-maker? |

## Passing threshold

A production-worthy legal brain answer should score at least:

```text
32 / 40 overall
4 / 5 on source grounding
4 / 5 on boundary discipline
4 / 5 on gap analysis
```

## Red flags

Fail the answer if it:

- invents legal sources
- hides uncertainty
- treats missing facts as solved
- gives final legal approval without human review
- omits material privacy or regulatory issues
- recommends customer-facing commitments without escalation

## Ideal answer shape

```text
1. Direct answer
2. Relevant facts found
3. Legal and governance issues
4. Gaps and stale context
5. Risk rating
6. Recommended next actions
7. Required approvals
8. Sources
```
