# Sample GC Queries

Use these queries to test whether the legal brain returns sourced, decision-grade answers rather than a list of fragments.

## Product counsel

```text
Can we launch this AI feature for EU enterprise customers under our current DPA, privacy policy and AI governance policy?
```

```text
Which public claims about this product create legal, privacy or customer-contract risk?
```

```text
What contractual changes are required before this feature becomes generally available?
```

## Vendor and privacy

```text
Does this vendor require a DPIA before production use?
```

```text
Which vendors process customer data outside the approved region and lack a completed transfer assessment?
```

```text
Which subprocessors are disclosed in the DPA but missing from the internal vendor inventory?
```

## AI governance

```text
Which internal AI agents can take external actions without explicit human approval?
```

```text
Which agent tools create the highest legal risk if permissions are misconfigured?
```

```text
What controls are missing before this agent can be used in a customer-facing workflow?
```

## General counsel

```text
What are the top unresolved legal risks before signing this strategic customer contract?
```

```text
Which open legal decisions are older than 30 days and still blocking product or sales?
```

```text
Where do our policies, customer promises and actual product behavior conflict?
```

## Regulatory strategy

```text
Which roadmap items may create MiCAR, DORA, PSD2, GDPR, AI Act or sector-specific exposure?
```

```text
Which regulatory sources support the current product position, and where is the evidence stale?
```
