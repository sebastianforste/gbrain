# Legal Company Brain Query Patterns

These patterns translate legal operations work into reusable company-brain queries.

## 1. Product launch review

```text
For [product or feature], retrieve the latest product facts, prior legal decisions, privacy assessments, customer commitments and jurisdiction notes. Identify launch blockers, stale assumptions, missing facts, required approvals and escalation triggers.
```

## 2. Customer commitment check

```text
Before changing [feature, retention period, model behavior or data flow], identify any signed customer commitments, security addenda, DPAs, order forms or negotiation notes that restrict the change. Separate binding commitments from informal statements.
```

## 3. Vendor renewal review

```text
For [vendor], retrieve the DPA, security review, subprocessor list, data categories, prior negotiation history, renewal date and any open risk items. Identify stale checks and required legal, privacy or security approvals before renewal.
```

## 4. Regulatory horizon review

```text
For [regulatory topic], retrieve primary sources, internal memos, prior decisions, affected products, owners and deadlines. Identify what changed, what is uncertain, which products are affected and what needs escalation.
```

## 5. Board legal risk brief

```text
Prepare a board-level legal risk brief for [period]. Use open legal risks, incidents, regulatory changes, vendor risks, customer commitments and unresolved escalations. Produce an executive summary with evidence, owners and next actions.
```

## 6. AI governance review

```text
For [AI use case], retrieve model purpose, data categories, human review steps, logging, retention, vendor dependencies, user-facing disclosures and prior approvals. Identify governance gaps and required approvals.
```

## 7. Stale legal decision check

```text
Find legal decisions that rely on assumptions, product facts or regulatory sources older than [date]. Prioritize decisions affecting live products, customer commitments or regulated markets.
```

## 8. Escalation trigger search

```text
Find all open matters where escalation triggers are present but no owner, deadline or final decision is recorded.
```

## Output template

```text
Answer
Sources relied on
Facts
Assumptions
Missing information
Stale or conflicting context
Risk level
Escalation trigger
Recommended next action
Reusable playbook update
```
