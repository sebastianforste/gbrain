# Legal Engineering Portfolio Note

This fork adds examples for legal knowledge systems, general counsel workflows and structured review of AI-enabled company operations.

## Demonstrated capabilities

- Source-grounded legal memory
- Product counsel review workflows
- Vendor and DPIA review workflows
- AI governance review workflows
- Human approval checkpoints
- Legal context server design
- Evaluation rubrics for legal answers
